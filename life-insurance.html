What services does SMP offer?
We offer mortgages, life insurance, critical illness and income protection, estate planning, wills, and business protection, all arranged on a whole-of-market basis.

Types of Cover

your income and lifestyle.

We find the right income protection policy based on your occupation, income, and how long you'd need cover to kick in.

01

Short-Term Cover

Pays a monthly benefit for a fixed period, typically 1 or 2 years. A cost-effective option if your employer offers some sick pay.

Budget-friendly
02

Long-Term Cover

Continues paying until you return to work or reach retirement age. The most comprehensive form of income protection, ideal if you have dependants or a mortgage.

Most comprehensive
03

Self-Employed Cover

Designed specifically for sole traders and contractors who have no employer sick pay to fall back on. Your income is your business, protect it properly.

For self-employed
04

Executive Income Protection

Set up through your limited company, with premiums paid as a tax-deductible expense. Tax-efficient protection for company directors.

For directors
