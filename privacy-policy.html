Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 April 2026

Secure Mortgages & Protection Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Secure Mortgages & Protection Ltd is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA No. 912820). We provide whole-of-market advice on mortgages, life insurance, income protection, and estate planning.

What data we collect

When you submit our callback request form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your age
  • The service you are interested in (mortgages, life insurance, income protection, or estate planning)

We may also automatically collect anonymous technical data when you visit our site — such as your browser type, device, pages visited, and how you interact with the site — via cookies and tracking pixels (see "Cookies and tracking" below).

How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Contact you about the advice you requested
  • Provide you with whole-of-market financial recommendations tailored to your situation
  • Comply with our regulatory obligations under FCA rules
  • Improve our services and website performance

Our lawful basis for processing is your consent (given when you submit the form) and, where relevant, our legitimate interest in providing you the service you requested.

Who we share your data with

We only share your personal data with:

  • Our dedicated advisers, who handle your case internally
  • Approved service providers who help us operate (hosting on Netlify, lead routing via Twilio) under strict data-processing agreements
  • Regulated product providers (lenders, insurers) only where necessary to complete the service you requested, and only with your explicit authorisation

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers or marketers outside the SMP adviser network.

How long we keep your data

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services, and subsequently for up to 6 years as required by FCA rules and UK financial services regulations. After this period, your data is securely deleted.

Cookies and tracking

Our website uses the following:

  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — to measure the performance of our advertising campaigns and build audiences for retargeting. This may track anonymous page views and interactions.
  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (e.g. to remember if you have already seen our callback form).

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent the site from working correctly.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to regulatory retention requirements)
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Security

We protect your data with industry-standard encryption (HTTPS), access controls, and secure processing partners. No method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take every reasonable step to safeguard your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Significant changes will be communicated on our website.
